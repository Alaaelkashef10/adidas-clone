ABOUT ADIDAS

Through sport, we have the power to change lives

Our Story


Adidas is a global leader in the sporting goods industry, with a rich heritage rooted in sport and a passion for innovation. Founded in 1949 by Adolf "Adi" Dassler, our company has grown from a small workshop in Herzogenaurach, Germany, to become one of the world's most recognizable brands.


For over 70 years, we have been at the forefront of sports performance, creating products that help athletes of all levels achieve their goals. From the Olympics to World Cups, from professional stadiums to local parks, Adidas has been there every step of the way.


Our mission is simple: be the best sports brand in the world. We achieve this through a relentless focus on innovation, quality, and sustainability. Every product we create is designed to enhance athletic performance while minimizing our environmental impact.

Innovation & Technology

Innovation is at the heart of everything we do at Adidas. Our research and development teams work tirelessly to create cutting-edge technologies that push the boundaries of sports performance.

From Boost cushioning technology that provides unmatched energy return, to Primeknit uppers that offer a sock-like fit, we are constantly pushing the limits of what's possible in athletic footwear and apparel.

We also lead the industry in sustainability innovation. Our Parley collection, made from recycled ocean plastic, demonstrates our commitment to protecting the environment while delivering high-performance products.

Commitment to Sustainability

We recognize that the future of sport depends on a healthy planet. That's why we've made sustainability a core part of our business strategy. By 2025, we aim to use only recycled polyester in all our products.

Our sustainable initiatives include reducing plastic waste, using renewable energy in our facilities, and creating products designed for recyclability. We believe that the best product is the one that doesn't harm the planet.
