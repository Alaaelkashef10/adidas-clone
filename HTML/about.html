Our Story


Adidas is a global leader in the sporting goods industry, with a rich heritage rooted in sport and a passion for innovation. Founded in 1949 by Adolf "Adi" Dassler, our company has grown from a small workshop in Herzogenaurach, Germany, to become one of the world's most recognizable brands.


For over 70 years, we have been at the forefront of sports performance, creating products that help athletes of all levels achieve their goals. From the Olympics to World Cups, from professional stadiums to local parks, Adidas has been there every step of the way.


Our mission is simple: be the best sports brand in the world. We achieve this through a relentless focus on innovation, quality, and sustainability. Every product we create is designed to enhance athletic performance while minimizing our environmental impact.

At Adidas, we believe that through sport, we have the power to change lives. This belief drives everything we do, from the products we design to the communities we support.
